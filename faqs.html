Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get face cards?

Face Cards are $10 to update and will be done by contacting the commissioner.

Are there individual awards?

Season 1 will not have individual awards, but very likely will have rewards in the future.

What do you get for winning?

The payout is indicative of the number of teams playing.

How do we receive the money?

The commissioner will contact the team leader and pay him/her.

How much does 2nd and 3rd place receive?

Rewards will only go towards the champions for this season. No rewards will be given to anyone else.

How long is the season?

Season 1 is two weeks long. Playoffs will be between 1 -2 weeks.

How can I become playoff eligible?

Teams must play a total of at least 10 games to be playoff eligible.

Why did my stats not show up?

Stats will only work if the screenshot sent into the Discord is in perfect formatting. There are factors that would cause this such as the image being blurry, too large/small, cut off, etc. The team record is still updated regardless of screenshot format, unless it is taken via camera. We will NOT accept those images.

How long until my stats show up?

It takes 24 hours for stats to be processed.

Is there a schedule?

Teams are responsible for scheduling their own games.
