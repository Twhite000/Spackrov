Spackrov League Rules

1. Schedule

Season 1 Regular Season (Feb 16 – Mar 1)
Season 1 Playoffs (Mar 2 – Mar 12)
Minimum games for playoff eligibility: 10
Team Standings: Wins / Games Played / Win %
Only games with proper screenshot evidence count.

Z
2. League Authority

Commissioner has final say.
Rules may be modified retroactively.
By joining, you accept all decisions.

O
3. Team & Players

Players can only be on one team.
Additional players cost $15; swaps cost $20.
Fees must be paid and communicated before the game.
Gamertags must be legitimate.
Roster changes must be verified.

N
4. Communication & Disputes

Chain: Players → Team Captain → Commissioner.
Disputes: Record games; evidence required.

X
5. Game Setup & Technical Issues

Up to 3 games per matchup; extras are for fun.
Scheduling is in Eastern Time.
Lag Out: Follow restart/quit rules (screenshots required).

B
6. Technical Requirements

VOD tutorial: Watch Here
Teams need a Discord rep.
Team logo must be on the court (PSN: Twhite000).
No refunds.

C
7. Regular Season Rules

Box scores submitted by the end of the last day.
Submit high-quality, formatted screenshots to Discord.
Discord is the primary scheduling method.

U
8. Playoffs

Top 16 teams qualify.
Each round is best-of-3 (same night).
Seeding determines game settings; leaving without consent concedes the series.

L
9. Violations

1st 2 violations: warning/forfeit.
3rd violation: removal (no refund).
Evidence required; report in Discord's "Game Violations" channel.
Starting with a known violation means acceptance.
