Spackrov League Rules

1. Schedule

Season 1 Regular Season (February 16 – March 1)
Season 1 Playoffs (March 2 – March 12)
Minimum games for playoff eligibility: 10 games
Team Standings are based on a combination of Wins, Games Played, and Win Percentage (in that order).
Only games with properly formatted screenshots will count – forfeit wins without a played game are not accepted, and stats count only when the screenshots are correct.

Z
2. League Authority

Commissioner has final say on all decisions.
Rules can be modified retroactively for fairness.
By joining, you accept all commissioner decisions.

O
3. Team & Players

Players can only be on one team.
If you did not sign up with 10 players, every additional player is $15 and a player swap is $20.
Extra player fees must be paid before the game and communicated with the commissioner – if not, the win will go to the opponent.
All gamertags must be legitimate and registered.
All roster changes must be verified before games.
All participating players must be on the team’s roster.

N
4. Communication & Disputes

1. Chain of Command:
Complaints/Comments should follow this structure: Players → Team Captain → Commissioner.
Teams are expected to resolve minor issues themselves.

2. Dispute Resolution:
It is highly recommended to record your games.
Teams must provide video or screenshot evidence.
The Commissioner will not track down proof.
Score disputes require screenshot proof.
Schedule conflicts must be documented in Discord.

X
5. Game Setup & Technical Issues

A team will only play another team up to 3 times. Any additional game is considered “for fun.”
All scheduling is in Eastern Time unless explicitly stated otherwise.

Lag Out Procedures:
- Before tipoff: Automatic restart.
- In the first quarter with less than a 10-point difference: Restart.
- If a lag out occurs past the 1st quarter or when the lead is 10 points or more, the team can quit within 30 seconds to finish the game. The scores and times are based on the moment of lag out, and the team must play the remaining time with the same score differential. If a team cannot quit due to 2k timing out the player, that team will play without the lagged-out player. The Commissioner will ultimately decide the fate of the game.
- You WILL NOT change your player if a lag out occurs – you must play with the same player that started the game.
- A screenshot of the time of lag out and a screenshot of the final time are required for this to apply.
- Example A: If Player 1 lags out at the end of the 1st quarter down 15-3, screenshot the scoreboard. Teams will play until the end of the 3rd quarter and then screenshot the scoreboard again.
- Example B: If Player 1 lags out in the 1st quarter down 15-6, the game will be restarted. Stats and time will not carry over, and the new game will be played fully.

B
6. Technical Requirements

VOD setup tutorial available: Watch Here.
Teams must have a representative in Discord.
The team logo must be on the court (find via PSN: Twhite000).
No refunds issued.

C
7. Regular Season Rules

Box scores must be submitted before the end of the night on the last day of the regular season.
Submit high-quality screenshots to Discord (no blurry images or TV photos).
Screenshots must be in the correct format (see Discord for examples).
Discord is the primary communication method for scheduling.

U
8. Playoffs

The top 16 teams will qualify for the playoffs.
Each round is a best-of-3 series, completed on the same night.
Game Settings:
The higher seed decides search and home/away for games 1 and 3.
The lower seed decides for game 2 (must communicate with the opponent beforehand).
Leaving without consent equals a series concession.

L
9. Violations

First 2 violations: Warning or forfeit.
Third violation: Removal from the league (no refund).
Proof of violations (video/screenshots) must be provided.
Report violations in Discord's "Game Violations" channel.
Starting a game with a known violation implies acceptance of the results.
